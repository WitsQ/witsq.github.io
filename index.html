Getting to the Venue

Getting onto Campus

To get onto the university campus, you should enter through one of the Yale Road entrances. There are numerous signs giving directions to buildings, as well as maps. Please ensure you have your ticket with you when you enter as you may be asked by security for proof of your attendance.

Lectures and Labs

All lectures and talks will be held in the Wits Science Stadium (WSS) on West Campus. Registration on the first day will also take place here. This will be the venue for all summer school days, though labs will be hosted in the Mathematical Sciences Labs.

Accommodation

If your accommodation was organised through the summer school, you will be staying on the university campus. Please arrive sometime on Sunday the 1st. Some organisers will be waiting for you at the Tower of Light on West Campus.

Getting Around

Travelling around Johannesburg, and to and from the O.R. Tambo airport, can be done using numerous public transport options in Johannesburg. The suggested way of getting from the airport to Johannesburg is the Gautrain. Click here for more suggested travelling methods.




More Information

Weather

It is currently summer in South Africa with an average high of 25 degrees Celsius, and low of 18 degrees Celsius. Please note that the laboratories are air conditioned, so it would be advisable to carry a light sweater.

Meals

A light lunch pack will be provided for the day delegates. If you have booked accommodation at the university residences, breakfast and dinner will be available.

Preschool Checklist





Entering Campus

Please enter the University of the Witwatersrand from one of the Yale road enterances (Empire Road and Jorrisen Street). To gain access to the campus, you will need to show your Quicket Ticket to the guards.

Safety

Please do not leave your personal belongings unattended in the conference rooms. You are responsible for your belongings at all times. When leaving your seat, please take your valuables with you. It would not be advisable to walk outside of the university campus unless with others.

First Day

If you are a delegate staying at the university residence, please make your way to the Tower of Light on the 1st of December, where you will be welcomed by some organisers. If you are a day delegate, please navigate to the Wits Science Stadium on the first day of the summer school.
